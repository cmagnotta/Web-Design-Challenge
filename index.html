WEATHER VISUALIZATION



What is the relationship between weather and latitude? Is the temperature higher closer to the equator? Do cities closer to the equator have less cloud cover? Are they more humid or windier? Weather data from 546 cities was collected into a database to cosider this question. In this site you will find four visualizations of that data created using Matplotlib.
